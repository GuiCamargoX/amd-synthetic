Detection of Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD)

The advances accomplished in the field of medicine provided the global population the opportunity to enjoy longer lives. Such a phenomenon is responsible for a growing number of elderly, directly impacting the increasing number of people suffering from age-related diseases. Among such conditions, one can refer to visual loss due to macular degeneration (AMD), which affected approximately 200 million people worldwide by 2020, and the number is expected to rise to 288 million individuals until 2040.
